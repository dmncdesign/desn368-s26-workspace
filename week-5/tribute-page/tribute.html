About

Butch Walker is an American musician. In the late 80’s he was lead guitarist for the hair band SouthGang, and joined members Jayce Fincher and Mitch “Slug” McLee in the alternative rock power pop band Marvelous 3. Marvelous 3 lasted from 1997 until 2001 due to record label problems, but reunited in 2023, doing shows around every Halloween. Butch joined Train as a lead guitarist in 2025. Butch also has many solo albums, ranging from a hard rock sound to a more pop sound, continuing to tour. Butch is also a well-known producer who has produced for tons of big artists, starting in the 2000’s and continuing through now.
